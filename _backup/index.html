Thesis & Research

Here is a brief on my recent academic research work.

Efficacy of Large Language Models in Facilitating Exam Preparation for Medical Students

As part of my undergraduate thesis, our team developed a dedicated MBBS dataset – the first of its kind in Bangladesh – and evaluated the performance of 11 prominent Large Language Models (LLMs). The goal was to assess how these models could assist medical students in preparing for their professional exams.

To enhance the effectiveness of study material generation, we implemented a Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) pipeline that enables automatic generation of MCQ-style questions and answers tailored to the MBBS curriculum.

  • First MBBS-specific dataset in Bangladesh
  • Evaluated 11 popular Large Language Models
  • Developed a RAG-based question-answer generation system
  • Targeted at improving exam preparation for medical students
